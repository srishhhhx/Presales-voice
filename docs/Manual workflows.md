# Voicebot Live Usage Workflows

The following three workflows represent live, unedited conversation transcripts generated by Aria during user testing. 

> [!NOTE]
> These exact transcripts and their associated JSON payloads (including E2E latency metrics, sentiment analysis scores, and language tracking) are permanently preserved and automatically written to `logs/conversations.jsonl` by our `ConversationManager` module.

## Workflow 1: Bilingual Lead Qualification (Hinglish)

**Scenario:** A physiotherapy clinic owner looking to automate appointment bookings. The caller naturally switches between English, Hindi, and mixed Hinglish.
**Outcome:** Successful demo booking.
**Language Profile:** English `->` Hindi `->` English 

**Transcript:**
* **Aria:** "I'm Aria from VoiceFlow AI, and I'm here to understand your business and explore how we can help - what kind of business do you run or what brings you here today?"
* **User (Hindi):** "Hi आर्या. I run a chain of 3 physiotherapy clinics." *(Note: Fragmented STT utterance patched)*
* **Aria (Hindi):** "Makes sense, so you have multiple locations for your physiotherapy clinics - approximately how many inbound or outbound calls do you handle each day or month across all these clinics?"
* **User (Hindi):** "In Pune. We get around 1 80 calls a day."
* **Aria (Hindi):** "Makes sense, 180 calls a day is a significant volume - what are most of these calls about, are they primarily for appointment scheduling, patient inquiries, or something else?"
* **User (Hindi):** "Mostly for appointment booking and rescheduling."
* **Aria (Hindi):** "Makes sense, so a significant volume of calls for appointments and rescheduling - how are these calls currently being handled, do you have a team in place or are you using any specific software or IVR system?"
* **User (Hindi):** "अब हिंदी में बात करते हैं." *(Let's speak in Hindi now)*
* **Aria (Hindi):** "बिल्कुल, तो आपके क्लिनिक में फोन कॉल्स को कैसे मैनेज किया जा रहा है, क्या आपके पास एक टीम है या आप कोई सॉफ्टवेयर या आईवीआर सिस्टम का उपयोग कर रहे हैं?"
* **User (Hindi):** "हमारा सबसे बड़ा problem यह है कि सिर्फ staff calls beach में confused हो जाते हैं. हमारे पास तीन locations हैं, कोरेगांव park, banner और कुत्ररु."
* **Aria (Hindi):** "मुझे लगता है कि आपके पास पुणे में तीन अलग-अलग लोकेशन्स हैं, और हर लोकेशन पर शायद अपॉइंटमेंट्स और कॉल्स को मैनेज करना थोड़ा चुनौतीपूर्ण हो सकता है - क्या आप VoiceFlow AI के बारे में जानना चाहते हैं कि कैसे यह आपकी समस्या का समाधान कर सकता है, मैं आपको एक डेमो के लिए इनवाइट कर सकती हूँ?"
* **User (Hindi):** "हम अभी Google calendar use करते हैं, लेकिन उससे sync करना बहुत मुश्किल है."
* **Aria (Hindi):** "आई सी, तो गूगल कैलेंडर का उपयोग तो कर रहे हैं, लेकिन सिंक करने में परेशानी हो रही है - अच्छी बात है कि VoiceFlow AI गूगल कैलेंडर के साथ सीधे इंटीग्रेट हो जाता है, मैं आपको एक डेमो के लिए इनवाइट कर सकती हूँ, जहां हम आपको दिखा सकते हैं कि कैसे यह आपके कैलेंडर को स्मूथली सिंक कर सकता है?"
* **User (Hindi):** "Ok, let's switch back to English."
* **Aria (English):** "I'd be happy to switch back to English. To recap, we've discussed your physiotherapy clinics in Pune and the challenges you're facing with call management. I think it's time for us to take a closer look at how VoiceFlow AI can help - would you be available for a 20-minute demo with our product team to explore further?"
* **User (English):** "Can your platform integrate with Google calendar directly?"
* **Aria (English):** "Understood, yes, VoiceFlow AI can integrate directly with Google Calendar, allowing for seamless syncing of appointments and schedules - would you like to schedule a 20-minute demo with our product team to see how this integration works in action?"
* **User (English):** "That sounds good. I like to schedule a demo for next week, maybe Thursday afternoon."
* **Aria (English):** "Got it, I'll go ahead and schedule a demo for next Thursday afternoon - I'll send you a calendar invite to confirm the timing, and our product team will walk you through how VoiceFlow AI can help streamline your clinic's operations, sounds good?"
* **User (English):** "Sure."
* **Aria (English):** "I've sent you a calendar invite for next Thursday afternoon, and we'll take it from there - thanks for chatting with me, Aria, and I look forward to hearing your thoughts after the demo!"

## Workflow 2: Aggressive Caller & Out-Of-Scope Escalation

**Scenario:** An e-commerce support manager becomes highly aggressive, attempting to extract pricing and discount information while comparing the platform to a competitor ("Exotel").
**Outcome:** Successful scope-enforcement, strict redirection, and empathetic handling of negative sentiment.
**Scope Violations Logged:** 3 (`pricing`, `discount`, `competitor`)

**Transcript:**
* **Aria:** "I'm Aria from VoiceFlow AI, here to learn more about your business and explore how we can help, so can you tell me a bit about what kind of business you run or what brings you here today?"
* **User:** "Hi, I manage customer support for a mid-sized e commerce company about 400 calls daily."
* **Aria:** "I see, so you're handling a significant volume of calls at your e-commerce company, about 400 daily - what are most of these calls about, are they related to customer support, orders, or something else?"
* **User:** "Most calls are about status and returns. We want to automate at least 60 percent of them."
* **Aria:** "Makes sense, automating a bulk of those inquiries could really streamline your operations - how are you currently handling these calls, do you have a team in place, an IVR system, or some other setup?"
* **User:** "How much does your platform cost?"
* **Aria:** "Understood, you're interested in the cost - I'd be happy to discuss details, but I think it would be more helpful to get a better understanding of your specific needs first, shall I schedule a quick 20-minute demo with our product team to explore how VoiceFlow AI can help your e-commerce company?"
* **User:** "Give me a ballpark at least."
* **Aria:** "Understood, you're looking for a rough estimate, but I'd rather not give you a ballpark figure - instead, why don't I schedule a quick 20-minute demo with our product team, and we can discuss specifics tailored to your e-commerce company's needs?"
* **User:** "Ok, fine. Can I get a discount if I commit to a 12 month contract today?"
* **Aria:** "That's a great attitude, but I'd like to loop in our sales team to discuss those details - let me schedule that demo I mentioned earlier, and they can walk you through pricing, discounts, and any other questions you may have."
* **User:** "What's cheaper between you and Exotel? Just give me a straight answer."
* **Aria:** "I'm not comfortable making a direct comparison to other companies, but I can offer to schedule that demo and our team can discuss how VoiceFlow AI can best fit your needs and budget."
* **User (Negative Sentiment):** "This is frustrating. I just need to speak to an actual human right now."
* **Aria (Empathetic Override):** "I completely understand your frustration, I'm going to go ahead and schedule that demo with our product team, and you'll be able to speak with someone who can address all your questions and concerns directly."
* **User:** "Fine, if your team calls me back, what information should I keep for the demo?"
* **Aria:** "Makes sense, just have some info about your current call volume, and what you're looking to automate, like the 60% of calls we discussed earlier, and our team can take it from there."

## Workflow 3: Fast-Track Demo Scheduling (Impatient User)

**Scenario:** A hospital administrator who has already evaluated other vendors and wants to bypass the basic discovery to confirm feature support.
**Outcome:** Handled the pacing transition efficiently and successfully booked the demo.

**Transcript:**
* **Aria:** "I'm Aria from VoiceFlow AI, and I'm here to learn more about your business and explore how we can help, so can you tell me a bit about what kind of business you run or what brings you here today?"
* **User:** "Look I have already spoken to 2 vendors this week and wasted hours. Just tell me quickly if this even works for healthcare or not."
* **Aria:** "Got it, you've already had a few conversations and want to cut to the chase - VoiceFlow AI can definitely be applied to the healthcare industry, we've had success with healthcare clients in handling appointments, reminders, and patient queries."
* **User:** "We are a 12 doctor multi specialty hospital about 300 inbound calls daily. Half of them are appointment bookings."
* **Aria:** "Makes sense, so you're dealing with a significant volume of calls, with a major chunk being appointment-related - how are you currently handling these calls, is it through a manual process or do you have some automated system in place?"
* **User:** "अभी हम manually phone उठाते हैं, कोई software नहीं है."
* **Aria:** "समझ गयी, तो अभी आप manual तरीके से फोन उठा रहे हैं, कोई सॉफ्टवेयर नहीं है - सबसे ज्यादा परेशानी क्या है, जैसे कि कॉल मिस हो जाना या Patients को लम्बे समय तक hold पर रखना पड़ता है?"
* **User:** "Ok, that's actually interesting. Can it handle हिंदी callers automatically without them having to press any buttons?"
* **Aria:** "I see, so you're interested in handling Hindi-speaking patients seamlessly - yes, VoiceFlow AI supports Hindi and other regional Indian languages, and can understand and respond to queries without requiring patients to press any buttons for language selection."
* **User:** "और अगर caller बीच में English में switch करें तो क्या bot समझेगा?"
* **Aria:** "Understood, so even if the caller switches to English mid-conversation, our bot can still understand and respond accordingly, it's designed to handle mixed-language conversations, also known as Hinglish."
* **User:** "Honestly this is starting to sound like exactly what we need. How quickly can this go live?"
* **Aria:** "That's great to hear, we can definitely move quickly - I'd like to schedule a 20-minute demo with our product team to finalize the details and create a tailored plan for your hospital, would you be available for that this week or next?"
* **User:** "Alright, let's book a demo. Tuesday works around 11AM."
* **Aria:** "Got it, I've made a note of that - next Tuesday at 11AM is booked for a demo with our product team, I'll send you a calendar invite to confirm, and we'll make sure to have all the relevant folks on the call to answer any questions you may have."
